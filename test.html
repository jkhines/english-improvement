Spelling Reform Test

The man thought through the problem thoroughly, though he was tough and rough. He brought enough food and broke through the obstacles. It was a great breakthrough.

Anglish Translation Test

The government announced a new policy to reduce pollution and improve the environment. Scientists use advanced technology to study nature and discover new species.

Education is important for society. Students learn mathematics, science, and language. Universities provide opportunities for research and development.

Mixed Content Test

Although the journey was difficult, they thought it was important to continue. The rough terrain made it tough, but they broke through and achieved their objective.

Instructions

  1. Open the browser console (F12) to see debug messages
  2. Click the extension icon
  3. Select "Spelling" or "Anglish" mode
  4. Click "Enable"
  5. Watch the text above transform
  6. Check console for: "English Improvement: Applied Anglish translation" or similar messages

Expected Transformations

Spelling Reform:

  • through → thru
  • though → tho
  • thought → thawt
  • tough/rough → tuff/ruff
  • break → brake
  • great → grate

Anglish:

  • government → folkdom
  • policy → way
  • reduce → lessen
  • pollution → befouling
  • environment → setting
  • technology → craftlore
  • university → highschool
